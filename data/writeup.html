Info and Conclusions

The original data set used is Mobility Report Cards: The Role of Colleges in Intergenerational Mobility. The original size is 85 columns and 2203 rows of data.

Data

Resources. (n.d.). Retrieved from https://opportunityinsights.org/paper/mobilityreportcards

Columns

  1. Count: Average number of kids per cohort.
  2. Average K Mean: The average earnings of each college student.
  3. Average Par Mean: The average earnings of each student's parents.
  4. Average Female: The average ratio of female students to male students.
